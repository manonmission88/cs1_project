Adventures Of Pinocchio
Author : Carlo Collodi
Publishing Date: 2009

  • NOVEL
  • FANTASY,CHILDREN'S LITERATURE

Pinocchio the character, is one of the great inventions of modern literature. A sublime anomaly, the book merges the traditions of the picaresque, of street theater, and of folk and fairy tales into a work that is at once adventure, satire, and a powerful enchantment that anticipates surrealism and magical realism. Pinocchio is an endlessly fascinating work that is essential equipment for life.
